Basic Tools

Version 1.0.3

An open-source, minimum-computing tool suite of everyday digital and computational utilities designed to run locally on your machine.

Basic Tools allows users to perform mundane yet necessary functionalities without the need of AI agents or AI programs, cloud storage spaces, proprietary (and often costly) software licenses, internet connection, and user account creation. A tool designed to honor human independence and human actions in the digital space while addressing the lack of attention in the software industry toward creating better user experience.

Project Inception

Scenario 1: I use a MacBook. The MacBook search bar consistently underperforms (especially the search function for a specific directory).
Scenario 2: I take a lot of photos, especially when going on archival research trips. I need a way to easily convert them to different formats and name the photo files in specific ways. There does not exist an efficient workflow that doesn't require expensive Adobe subscription or AI agent.
